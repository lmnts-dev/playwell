The Importance of STEM Education
Building a Better Future

STEM education creates critical thinkers, increases science literacy, and enables the next generation of innovators. Innovation leads to new products and processes that sustain our economy in an ever increasing technologically driven society.

Having activities that show real-life implication of STEM can pull together the ideas presented in school and help to show how they benefit our society and even our world as a whole

Our impact on STEM Education
Children Learn While Playing

Our assessments measured three goals including: student's knowledge of STEM terms or concepts, ability to generalize information, and increasing positive feeling towards STEM subjects. For example, the definition of gravity or friction, or that when two gears interlock their teeth its called meshing.
